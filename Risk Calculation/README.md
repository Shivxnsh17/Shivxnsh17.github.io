# Risk Calculation & Analysis

This directory contains Excel models dedicated to evaluating stock performance, fundamentals, ratio analysis, and risk metrics, often benchmarked against the Nifty 50 index.

## Files Overview

### 1. [Multi Industry Stock Analysis.xlsx](./Multi%20Industry%20Stock%20Analysis.xlsx)
Provides a comparative analysis across multiple industries focusing on fundamentals and key metrics.
**Key Sheets:**
- `TCS`, `Max Healthcare`, `Tata Power`, `Hindustan Unilever`, `State Bank of India`, `FUNDAMENTALS`, `Financials`

### 2. [Stocks Vs. Nifty 50 Fundamentals.xlsx](./Stocks%20Vs.%20Nifty%2050%20Fundamentals.xlsx)
Compares the fundamentals of specific stocks against the broader Nifty 50 index using moving averages and standard deviations.
**Key Sheets:**
- `Reliance Industries`, `Adani Energy Sol.`, `TCS`, `HDFC Bank`, `SBI Bank`, `DLF`, `Tata Steel`, `Vedanta`, `Cipla`, `NIFTY 50`, `Analysis`

### 3. [Stocks Vs. Nifty 50 Ratio Analysis.xlsx](./Stocks%20Vs.%20Nifty%2050%20Ratio%20Analysis.xlsx)
A detailed risk and return ratio analysis (Sharpe, Treynor, Jensen's Alpha, Max Drawdown) comparing stocks to the market index.
**Key Sheets:**
- `Axis_Bank`, `Power_Grid`, `Coal_India`, `Google_Alphabet`, `Cipla`, `Shriram_Finance`, `Larsen_and_Toubro`, `Nifty_50`, `ANALYSIS`

### 4. [Stocks Vs. Nifty 50.xlsx](./Stocks%20Vs.%20Nifty%2050.xlsx)
A high-level comparison and risk assessment of selected stocks against Nifty 50.
