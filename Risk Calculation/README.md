# Risk Calculation & Analysis

This directory contains Excel models dedicated to evaluating stock performance, fundamentals, ratio analysis, and risk metrics, often benchmarked against the Nifty 50 index.

## Models

- [[Multi Industry Stock Analysis]] - Provides a comparative analysis across multiple industries focusing on fundamentals and key metrics.
- [[Stocks Vs. Nifty 50 Fundamentals]] - Compares the fundamentals of specific stocks against the broader Nifty 50 index using moving averages and standard deviations.
- [[Stocks Vs. Nifty 50 Ratio Analysis]] - A detailed risk and return ratio analysis (Sharpe, Treynor, Jensen's Alpha, Max Drawdown) comparing stocks to the market index.
- [[Stocks Vs. Nifty 50]] - A high-level comparison and risk assessment of selected stocks against Nifty 50.
